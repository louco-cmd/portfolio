Taxes declaration

Revamping Impôt.gouv

This project is focused on improving the official French tax website, Impôt.gouv, by modernizing its design, enhancing user interaction, and making it more intuitive. The goal is to simplify tax declaration and access to government services for French citizens, making the experience smoother and more efficient.

With a complete redesign of the interface, we aim to make taxes less intimidating and more user-friendly for all. Click below to see the design process!

Design steps

The design process for revamping Impôt.gouv involved several key stages, each aimed at refining user experience and ensuring accessibility for everyone.

1.

Research

In this phase, I analyzed the existing user experience conducting an ergonomic review

2.

Sketching

I began sketching ideas based on the research findings to create wireframes and map out the new site layout.

3.

Prototyping

I developed prototypes to test the new design concepts and gathered feedback before moving on to development.

Research - Ergonomic review

My ergonomic review focused on identifying areas where the website could be more user-friendly. I evaluated the site’s layout, ease of navigation, and accessibility features to improve the overall user experience.
