TotalEnergies Singapore

Institutional website revamp

TotalEnergies Singapore serves as the hub for Asia Pacific and the Middle East (APME), representing all the company’s activities in the region. A recent technical migration to Drupal 7 reignited interest in this digital asset, leading to the decision to revamp the website with three primary objectives:

  • Present Singapore as a hub for all regional activities.
  • Emphasize the "One Total" strategy, unifying all branches under one digital roof.
  • Leverage digital tools to enhance brand communication and business outcomes.

Structure definition with card sorting

To ensure the website's information architecture aligned with user needs, we conducted card-sorting sessions with stakeholders and end-users. This method allowed us to categorize content logically, ensuring intuitive navigation across the site.
