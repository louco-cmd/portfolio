GARI, a LLM designed to support the bank concellor

GARI is an AI-powered assistant designed to help bank advisors manage their daily tasks more effectively. By utilizing advanced AI and Natural Language Processing (NLP), GARI offers real-time support to the advisors, helping them with tasks such as answering internal queries, automating repetitive actions, and providing quick insights on client data. This allows advisors to focus on high-value interactions with clients, improving their productivity and decision-making.

Feature ideation

Feature Ideation

UI kit

During the ideation phase, we focused on creating features that would simplify the bank advisor's routine. Key functionalities included an AI-powered assistant for internal queries, a task automation engine to handle repetitive work, and an intelligent alert system that notifies advisors of important updates or client information. We aimed to develop tools that enhance productivity without overwhelming the advisors with complex interfaces, ensuring ease of use and relevance.

User testing

User testing - User diary

To ensure that GARI met the practical needs of bank advisors, we conducted user testing in the form of diary studies. Advisors were asked to use GARI over an extended period, logging their experiences and providing feedback on its utility in their daily tasks. This hands-on feedback helped us refine the system, ensuring that it was both effective in supporting their work and easy to use in a fast-paced environment.
