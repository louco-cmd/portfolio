GEDEO
dashboard

Manager dashboard for a process management tool

The GEDEO dashboard simplifies internal process management by providing team leaders with real-time access to key data, monitoring tools, and progress trackers. Its user-friendly interface enables quick, informed decisions, boosting workflow efficiency.

The tool centralizes operational metrics, offering insights into project statuses, team performance, and task progress. With a responsive design, it promotes collaboration and enhances overall productivity.

Design steps

The design process for revamping GEDEO involved several key stages, each aimed at refining user experience and ensuring accessibility for everyone.

1.

Research and ideation

We began by understanding user needs and exploring different ideas to improve the dashboard’s functionality and user experience.

2.

Features priorization

Based on the research, we identified and prioritized key features to focus on, ensuring the most impactful elements were developed first.

3.

Sketching

We created wireframes and prototypes to visualize the layout and design, refining the user flow and ensuring an intuitive interface.

Research

Our research revealed that managers were struggling with fragmented data and slow reporting tools. We focused on understanding how data could be presented more clearly, improving efficiency in decision-making. We also worked on identifying pain points in the user journey, such as navigating through outdated reports and information.

Priorization

Prioritization

During the UX research phase, numerous innovative ideas emerged. To ensure the product's success, we implemented a structured approach to prioritize features. Guided by the Product Owner's strategic vision, we utilized the MoSCoW prioritization matrix to identify the "must-haves," "should-haves," "could-haves," and "won’t-haves." This methodology helped us strike the perfect balance between aligning with the product strategy and addressing user needs, resulting in a dashboard that is both impactful and user-centric.
