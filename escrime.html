Fencing club

Institutional website revamp

This personal project is focused on revamping the institutional website for a fencing sporting club. The aim is to update its design and enhance user experience.

The redesign includes creating a modern, visually appealing interface with intuitive navigation to improve accessibility for members and visitors.

See case study

Design steps

The design process for revamping the website involved several key stages to ensure both functionality and aesthetic appeal.

1.

Branding review

Reviewed the current branding to align the new design with the club’s identity while introducing fresh, modern design elements..

2.

Sketching

Developed wireframes and layouts to establish the site's structure and ensure ease of use for all visitors.

3.

Prototyping

Created an interactive prototype to visualize the final design and test its functionality with real users.

Ergonomic review

Performed a thorough ergonomic review of the website’s layout to identify any usability issues. Focused on simplifying navigation and ensuring that information was easy to find and interact with.
